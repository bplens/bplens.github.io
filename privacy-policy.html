BP Lens Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2025

1. Introduction

BP Lens ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our productivity application for digitizing numerical readings.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Data

We use your device's camera to capture numerical readings from physical displays. The camera is only used when you actively choose to digitize a reading. The captured images are processed for text recognition using artificial intelligence services. For quality improvement purposes, we may store anonymized images on our servers for up to 1 month before deletion. These stored images are not tied to any individual user.

2.2 OCR Data

We use artificial intelligence services for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) processing. Currently, this is provided by OpenAI's GPT service. The images are processed for text recognition. For quality improvement, we may store anonymized images for up to 1 month before deletion. These stored images are not tied to any individual user.

2.3 Storage Access

We access your device storage only when you choose to import or export your records.

3. Advertising

We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data as described in their privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your digitized readings are stored locally on your device. For quality improvement purposes, we may store anonymized images on our servers for up to 1 month before deletion:

  • All personal data is stored only on your device
  • Images may be stored temporarily for service improvement
  • Stored images are anonymized and deleted within 1 month
  • You have complete control over your personal data

5. Data Export

You can export your data in CSV format. You are responsible for the security of exported data files.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • OpenAI GPT for OCR processing

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
